About us

This project was developed by a group of 4th Year Computer Engineering Students as a final requirement for their course, CPE Elective 3AIDA – Deep Learning. This web application serves as a recommendation system for the LGUs of Las Piñas in determining all of the possible vaccination sites along with the city, considering the utmost convenience for the people. The problem was methodically identified through interviews, observations, numerous documentation, and surveys of the people from all over the NCR Region; however, due to limited time frame and resources, the TSDC group was only able to develop solutions for the city of Las Piñas only.
